What is Startup Weekend Busan?

This is a special competition in which groups of 2-4 get together and build a startup in a weekend. On Sunday evening, groups will present their startup to a panel of judges. 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place winners will be announced.

How will it be conducted?

The groups will be given a guideline (theme) for the startup. They have the weekend to work on their idea. Presentations will be on Sunday evening.
